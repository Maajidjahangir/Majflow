How it works

From zero to client —
in 5 automated steps.

One pipeline. Runs every Monday. You only show up to close.

1
Apify scrapes 300+ leads
Every Monday at 9am, Apify automatically pulls targeted leads from LinkedIn, Google Maps, or any directory. Company name, website, decision-maker profile — all in one clean CSV.
⚡ Automated
2
Snov.io finds & verifies emails
Every lead is enriched with a verified business email. Bounces are filtered out before outreach begins — protecting your sender reputation.
⚡ Automated

Instantly.ai sends personalised sequences
Verified leads are enrolled in a 5-step email sequence over 12 days. AI detects positive replies and flags them instantly — no manual inbox monitoring needed.

The moment someone replies positively, a CRM contact is created, a WhatsApp message fires to qualify them, and a discovery call gets booked — all without you touching a thing.

FAQ

Questions we get asked.

Do I need any technical knowledge?
+
Absolutely not. We handle 100% of the setup — Apify, Snov.io, Instantly.ai, GoHighLevel, MessengerFlow, and Make.com. You don't touch a single line of code. Once live, the system runs itself.
How long does setup take?
+
Full pipeline setup takes 7–10 days. Email warm-up (mandatory for deliverability) runs in parallel for 14 days. You'll see first results in week 2 after launch.
Do I need to buy the tools separately?
+
Yes — the tools are third-party SaaS products you subscribe to directly (~$254/month total). We handle setup and configuration. This keeps you in control of your own data and accounts.
Is cold email legal in India?
+
B2B cold email outreach is legal in India and most countries. We follow best practices — verified emails, unsubscribe links, compliant messaging — to ensure your campaigns are both legal and effective.
What niche or industry works best?
+
The pipeline works across most B2B niches — digital marketing, consulting, SaaS, real estate, HR, web development, and more. Local business targeting via Google Maps works exceptionally well for India.
What if I don't get results?
+
We optimise the campaign — subject lines, targeting, sequences — until it works. Growth and Agency plans include ongoing support. Results depend on niche and offer quality, but we've never had a pipeline that didn't generate replies.
Can I scale after setup?
+
Yes — scaling means adding more domains, more lead sources, and more Instantly.ai seats. The Make.com automation handles increased volume automatically. We've designed the system to scale without proportional cost increases.
Do you offer ongoing management?
+
Yes. Our Agency plan includes 3 months of monthly optimisation calls. We also offer standalone retainer packages for businesses who want continuous campaign management and lead flow improvement.
